Chicken Katsu

Breaded chicken fried in oil. It is covered in tonkatsu sauce. I like to server it with broccoli. It pairs nicely with white rice.

Ingredients

  • panko
  • chicken
  • ketchup
  • oyster sauce
  • soy sauce
  • egg

Steps

  1. beat the chicken until it is think and tender
  2. beat the egg and bread chicken by dipping in the egg before dredging through panko
  3. fry on medium high heat 3min per side
