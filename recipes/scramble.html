Breakfast Scramble

Description

A tasty and quick breakfast scramble. This should take no longer then 20 minutes and is great for when you have a little bit extra time before work.

Ingredients

  • Red Bellpepper
  • potatoes (diced)
  • red onion
  • garlic
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. dice onions and bellpeppers
  2. fry onions and bellpeppers over medium heat with salt pepper and garlic
  3. once onions are translucent add egg and scramble
