Delicious Feta and Tomato Pasta

How does it taste??

This is a delicious recipe I found on reddit. It's tangy tasty and quick. This recipe will be quick. A simple 5 minutes prep time and 15 minutes of baking is all you need!

Ingredients

  • 8oz Feta Cheese
  • Box of Toy Box Tomato's
  • 4oz Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano

Directions

  1. Add oil to pan
  2. Add whole and unbroken feta cheese to the center of the pan
  3. Surround the feta with the toy box tomatoes
  4. Add salt pepper and oregano to taste
  5. bake at 350F for 25 minutes or until tomatoes begin to split
  6. Let cool before smashing tomatoes and mixing feta cheese
  7. EAT!
